About this Project

What is it?
AI News for My.se Students is a web platform designed to provide Malmö Yrkeshögskola (My.se) students with fast, modern, and curated access to the latest news and trends in artificial intelligence and technology.

Why and for whom?
The goal is to bring technological innovation and AI closer to students, teachers, and enthusiasts at My.se, making learning and inspiration easier with up-to-date resources and a visually appealing experience.

How was it built?
  • HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript: For maximum compatibility, speed, and visual control, without heavy frameworks.
  • Inspired by The New York Times: A professional, clean, and readable aesthetic was sought, with clear menus and elegant typography.
  • Supabase: Used as a backend to store and display images in the gallery, allowing students to upload and share their art easily.
  • Vercel: The site is deployed on Vercel for high availability, speed, and a custom domain.
  • SEO & Shareability: Meta tags, sitemap, and robots.txt were added to improve search ranking and social media appearance.
  • Accessibility & Responsiveness: The design adapts to mobile and desktop, ensuring an optimal experience on any device.

This project is a foundation for other students to learn, experiment, and expand with new ideas, tools, and sections.
Inspired by the spirit of modern journalism and open education.
